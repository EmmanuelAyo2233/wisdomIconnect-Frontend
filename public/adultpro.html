Hi, I'm Joy! I’m a content writer and community management professional with over 5 years of experience. I specialize in crafting compelling content that resonates with diverse audiences and driving brand advocacy through effective community management.

I've had the pleasure of working with both international companies in Silicon Valley and local brands. One of my key strengths is cultivating and nurturing vibrant communities, both online and offline. I’ve managed communities ranging from fewer than 100 to over 10,000 members across platforms like Slack, Discord, Telegram, and Facebook.

As a Meta Certified Community Manager, I bring a data-driven approach to everything I do—whether it’s developing community guidelines, leading teams, creating content calendars, organizing engaging events, or measuring the impact of strategies to make informed decisions.

Outside of work, I love public speaking, moderating events, and reading books. I also enjoy sketching and painting to tap into my creative side. If you're interested in content creation, community building, or just want to chat about books or art, I’m here to help!
